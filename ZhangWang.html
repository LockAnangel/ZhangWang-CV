Biography

I am a postgraduate student in spatial-temporal database Group, Department of Computer Science, South-Central University for Nationalities. My supervisor is Prof. Yanhong Li. I received B.S. degree from Department of Computer in Hubei University of Education at 2016.

I have broad research interests in spatial information and communication, and multimedia network technology. I have dedicated to address pub/sub problem with Boolean expression in the Internet of Industrial Vehicles, and I also tried to answer Why-not questions on augmented spatial keyword Top-k queries and direction aware augmented spatial keyword Top-k queries. Recently I am focusing on answering Why-not questions on other types of queries such as graph queries and trajectory queries.
